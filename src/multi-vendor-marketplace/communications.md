# Communications 

Sellers and admins can communicate directly with each other through the admin panel, making collaboration and issue resolution more efficient. 

They can exchange messages, share attachments, and manage important announcements, ensuring that both parties stay informed and can address operational concerns quickly.

In addition to admin–seller communication, customers can also interact directly with sellers for product inquiries, support requests, and order-related discussions. 

This direct communication channel helps improve transparency, speeds up problem resolution, and enhances the overall customer experience on the platform.
