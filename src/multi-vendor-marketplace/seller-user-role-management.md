# Seller Users & Roles

Larger stores are rarely run by one person. The Multi Vendor Marketplace lets a seller add **team members (users)** to their store and control exactly what each member can do through **roles**. This keeps the store secure while letting sellers delegate day-to-day work.

Both screens live under the seller panel **Settings** menu.

## Roles

A role is a named set of permissions. Sellers create roles and assign them to their team members.

### Create a Role

Go to **Settings → Roles** and click **Create Role**, then provide:

- **Name:** A label for the role (e.g. *Catalog Manager*, *Order Staff*).

- **Description:** A short note describing what the role is for.

- **Permission Type:**
  - **All** — the role can access every seller-panel section.
  - **Custom** — pick exactly which sections the role may use from the permission tree (Products, Orders, Transactions, Settings, etc.). When you tick a permission, its parent sections are selected automatically so access stays consistent.

Click **Save** to create the role.

<ImagePopup src="/images/multi-vendor-marketplace/seller-role-create.png" alt="Create Seller Role" />

> **Tip:** Use **Custom** roles to give staff only what they need — for example, a role that can manage products but cannot view transactions or change store settings.

### Edit or Delete

Sellers can update a role's permissions at any time, or delete a role that is no longer needed. Changing a role from **All** to **Custom** re-applies the newly selected permission set to everyone holding that role.

## Users (Team Members)

Users are the people who log in to the seller store. Each user is assigned a role that defines their access.

### Add a User

Go to **Settings → Users** and click **Create User**, then provide:

- **Name:** The team member's name.

- **Email:** Their login email address (must be unique).

- **Password / Confirm Password:** Set the account password. (When editing an existing user, leaving the password blank keeps the current one.)

- **Role:** Choose one of the roles created above to control the user's access.

- **Status:** Enable or disable the account.

<ImagePopup src="/images/multi-vendor-marketplace/seller-user-create.png" alt="Create Seller User" />

Click **Save** to create the user. They can now sign in to the seller panel with the permissions granted by their role.

### Edit or Delete

Sellers can update a user's details, change their role, reset their password, or remove the user entirely.

> **Note:** The original seller account is the owner and always retains full access. Added users are sub-accounts whose access is limited by their assigned role.
