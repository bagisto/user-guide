# Seller Reporting

The Reporting section gives sellers clear, data-driven insight into how their store is performing. Sellers can track revenue trends, see which products sell best, and understand their customer base — all from one place in the seller panel.

### Where to find it

From the seller panel, go to the **Reporting** menu, which contains three reports:

- **Sales**
- **Products**
- **Customers**

Each report supports a **date range / time filter** so sellers can compare performance across periods, and an **Export** option to download the data.

### Sales Report

The Sales report summarises the store's revenue performance, including totals such as sales, orders, and average values over the selected period, with trends shown over time. Use it to spot growth, seasonality, and the impact of promotions.

### Products Report

The Products report highlights product-level performance — for example top-selling products and the quantities sold — helping sellers decide what to restock, promote, or discontinue.

### Customers Report

The Customers report focuses on the buyer side — such as customer activity and traffic over the selected period — so sellers can understand demand and repeat-purchase behaviour.

### Filtering & Export

- **Date filter:** Narrow any report to a specific range to analyse a campaign or compare months.

- **Export:** Download the report data for offline analysis or record-keeping.

> **Note:** Reporting figures are scoped to the signed-in seller's own store, so each seller sees only their own sales, products, and customers.
