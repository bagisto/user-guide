# Booking Management

Booking management lets sellers track and manage **booking-type products** — items sold as reservations, appointments, events, rentals, or time slots. Sellers can see every booking placed against their bookable products in one place.

### Where to find it

From the seller panel, go to **Sales → Bookings**.

The Bookings page presents the seller's bookings in two complementary views:

- **List (DataGrid) view:** A searchable, filterable table of all bookings, showing the order, product, customer, booking slot/date, quantity, and status.

- **Calendar view:** A calendar that plots bookings by date so the seller can see upcoming reservations at a glance and avoid scheduling conflicts.

### Managing Bookings

From the list, sellers can review the details of each booking — the linked order, the customer, the reserved slot, and its current status — and proceed with fulfilment (invoice/shipment) through the standard order flow under **Sales → Orders**.

> **Note:** Bookings appear here only for products created with the **Booking** product type. Configure the bookable options (slots, availability, pricing) on the product edit page when creating a booking product.
