# B2B eCommerce Platform 

The [B2B eCommerce Platform](https://bagisto.com/en/b2b-commerce-platform/) is an open-source solution that brings advanced Business-to-Business capabilities to the Bagisto eCommerce platform. It enables companies to manage corporate accounts, bulk orders, and team access from one place, with ease.

Built to streamline B2B operations, it allows businesses to register their company, manage multiple users, and handle the entire purchasing process from a single, unified dashboard.

Powerful tools such as Quick Order, Requisition Lists, and Request for Quote (RFQ) make buying faster, simpler, and better organized for business customers, even across large or repeat orders.

With role-based permissions, companies can assign roles and control exactly what each user can access, ensuring a secure, transparent, and well-managed workflow across the entire team.

The platform also gives administrators fine-grained control over each company's experience through **Company Catalogs** (per-company product and category visibility with custom and quantity-tier pricing), **Company Credit** (a buy-now-pay-later account with an audited ledger and a *Pay By Credit* checkout method), **Sales Representatives** (assign admin users to manage specific companies and their negotiations), and **Company Attributes** (extend the company profile with custom fields).

## Key Features

* **Company Registration & Approval** – Storefront company sign-up with optional admin approval before activation.
* **Role Based Permissions** – Custom company roles and granular access control for company users.
* **Company User Management** – Add company users directly or invite existing store customers to join.
* **Requisition Lists, Quick Order & Request for Quote** – Faster, better-organized bulk and repeat purchasing.
* **Quotation Handling** – End-to-end buyer–seller negotiation on both the storefront and admin.
* **Purchase Orders** – Track and manage procurement across the company.
* **Company Catalogs** – Per-company product and category visibility with custom and tier pricing.
* **Company Credit (Pay By Credit)** – Per-company credit limits with an audited ledger and reimbursements.
* **Sales Representatives** – Assign admin users to manage specific companies.
* **Company Attributes** – Custom fields on the company profile.
* **Localization & RTL** – Full right-to-left support and translations for all 22 supported locales.

This makes the B2B eCommerce Platform an ideal fit for wholesalers, manufacturers, and distributors looking to run a scalable, flexible, and efficient online store tailored to business buyers.
